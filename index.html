ProCure In Short

ProCure is a onestop solution to all of your healthcare problems. There are three variants of the ProCure mobile app. One for patients, one for doctors and one for pharmacists. The ProCure app uses blockchain technology to keep all of your previous and future prescriptions safe and secure. This eliminates the hassle of taking all of your previous files and prescriptions, test results to the new doctor. ProCure app asigns a unique ID and a digital signature to each user and creates a new blockchain for each individual. Data security and integrity is something that something that we consider as really important in 2021. ProCure is a secure platform for patients, doctors and pharmacists for safe prescription and medical report transactions.
